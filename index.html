Stuffed Shells

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Boil water in a large pot
  3. In a mixing bowl, mix togother ricotta cheese, grated paramesan chesse, basil, oregano and one egg.
  4. Mix all of the ingredients until they are combined together.
  5. Check stuffed shells to see if they are al-denta.
  6. Remove stuffed shells from large pot. Then, stuff shells with mixture.
  7. Place stuffed sheels in a baking pan and add tomato sauce.
  8. Once the tomato sauce has been added, add the fresh mozzarella on top.
  9. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.

Enjoy, this delicious pasta dish!
